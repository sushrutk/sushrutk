Sushrut Karmalkar

I am currently a graduate student at the University of Texas at Austin, advised by Prof. Adam Klivans.

My interests include theoretical computer science and machine learning theory.

Previously I was an undergraduate and then a masters student at the Chennai Mathematical Insititute, India.
